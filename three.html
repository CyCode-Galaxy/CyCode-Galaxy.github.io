Strong Passwords (Mixing Characters)

15 min

Why Do We Need Strong Passwords?

Passwords are like keys to your online world. A strong password keeps your accounts safe from hackers, just like a strong lock keeps your diary safe.

Security Tip

Never share your passwords with friends, even your best friend! Passwords should always be private.

What Makes a Password Strong?

A strong password has different types of characters mixed together:

Strong Password
D0gL0v3r#2023
  • Uppercase letters (D, L)
  • Lowercase letters (o, g, v, e, r)
  • Numbers (0, 3, 2, 0, 2, 3)
  • Special character (#)
Weak Password
dog123
  • Only lowercase letters
  • Simple numbers
  • Too short
  • Easy to guess
Password Builder Game

Create your own strong password using these rules:

  1. Start with your favorite animal (e.g., "Elephant")
  2. Change some letters to numbers (e.g., "E1ephant")
  3. Add a special character (e.g., "E1ephant!")
  4. Include the current year (e.g., "E1ephant!2023")

Password Do's and Don'ts

Do
  • Use at least 8 characters
  • Mix letters, numbers, and symbols
  • Create different passwords for important accounts
  • Tell an adult if you think someone knows your password
Don't
  • Use your name or birthday
  • Use "password" or "123456"
  • Share passwords with friends
  • Write passwords where others can see them
